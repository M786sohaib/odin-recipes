Odin Recipes

Here are a few recipes that I'd like to share in this first ever real project that I am working on.
  • Mutton Biryani

    The mutton biryani is a dish made of rice, spices, yogurt, and as it's name suggest mutton. The dish is very popular in many parts of India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh. Biryani is said to have originated in Hyderabad, India where it is almost synonimous to festical food.

  • Tuscan Butter Shrimp

    These Tuscan butter shrimp, in a delightful cherry tomato and sun-dried tomato cream sauce, can be served over pasta or polenta, or with a salad and toasted sourdough bread.

  • Beef Miso Ramen

    This flavorful bowl of miso ramen is full of delicious flavors that will make your palate sing! If you've been dreaming of Japanese cuisine, this is the perfect meal for you. Add a hard-boiled egg, mushrooms, spicy chili oil, or toppings of your choice.
